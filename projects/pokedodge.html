POKÉDODGE

A game created in Flash/ActionScript.

Repository
About

PokéDodge is a game created in Flash/Actionscript by myself and a friend for our highschool programming class. It served as a way to demonstrate to the teacher AND to ourselves what was possible with only two months worth of basic programming knowledge, on top of another two months of hard work. In the game, you play as a Pokémon controlled by a mouse or the WASD keys. The goal of the game is to dodge the rapidly spawning Pokéballs flying across the screen, while also being wary of the MasterBall that has character tracking abilities. An Options screen is also provided for the user so that they can customize the game and play to their liking.
